About Me

Hi, I'm Andy Le

Before I decided to pursue a career in software development, I graduated with a B.S. in Information Technology and Systems, later getting my M.S. in Information Technology and Management, and after working in my field I realized I felt that there was work that would fit my interests better. I always felt drawn to being able to create new experiences for people and make their lives easier. And I saw that software development was a great way to do that.


I decided to take a leap and enrolled in Bloomtech's Full Stack Web Development program. I have a strong foundation in JavaScript, React, and Node.js, and I'm always eager to learn new technologies and frameworks. My goal is to create innovative solutions that make a positive impact on people's lives. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow my skills. I have skills in web development, mobile app development, and software engineering, and I'm excited to share my work with you.
